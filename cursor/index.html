Creates a ~/sylvan folder with a track script and adds a Cursor rule so your AI auto-tracks your work.

What it does: As you work in Cursor, the AI automatically logs meaningful activities вҖ” decisions, breakthroughs, context switches, how you're feeling. Everything stays local as JSON files.

Over time you build a searchable log of what you did, why, and how you were feeling. Great for standups, retros, or just remembering what happened last Tuesday.

What gets created: A ~/sylvan folder with a track script (~50 lines, Python + bash) and a .cursor/rules/sylvan-track.mdc rule that tells Cursor's AI when and how to track. That's it. Take a look.
